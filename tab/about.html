About

QRCode Generator is a personal project I develop to help people turn any text such url, messages and code to scannable qrcode. Since it runs on the browser you can generate qrcode from any platform anytime you want.

Purpose

The main goal of QRCode Generator is to fulfill my univ assignment, but beside that this would work just fine to simply generate and download your qrcode.

Features

  • Easy Image Size Changer.
  • Download the generated qrcode.
  • History tab, to download your old generated qrcode.
